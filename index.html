About Me

I'm a passionate and driven game developer and aspiring full-stack software developer whose creativity knows no bounds in the digital realm. I’m also a proud father of two amazing sons and a grandfather to three energetic boys—they are my inspiration and motivation every day.

I recently completed The Tech Academy’s intensive Game Developer Boot Camp, where I trained in a wide range of modern programming and web development languages including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, SQL, C#, Python, and C++. Through hands-on experience and real-world projects, I gained a solid foundation in both front-end and back-end technologies.

My journey through game development has been exciting and deeply fulfilling. In Unity, I learned how to bring ideas to life by working with scenes, characters, game objects, scripts, animations, lighting, and particle effects. I created multiple games using Unity—most notably, Coins Gone Wild, a fun and energetic platformer where players hop around collecting coins, and a mini solar system simulation that demonstrates object motion and rotation. Both are available on my GitHub.

I also completed a C++ and Unreal Engine course, where I built a game called Coin Collector, which features randomly generated coins and glowing obstacle boxes within a grid. This project taught me a great deal about procedural generation, memory management, and the power of Unreal Engine's C++ backend. And this is just the beginning—my head is full of new game ideas I’m eager to build.

As I approach the completion of my full-stack training, I’m excited about the future. I love building things—from intuitive web applications to immersive game worlds—and I’m eager to bring that passion to a professional team. Whether it's games, tools, or software systems, I'm ready to contribute, collaborate, and continue growing.
