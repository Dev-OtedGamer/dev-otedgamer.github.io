My Story

I was taught at the age of 10 (86'-87') how to make basic games(animation) on the early macintosh computers at Shriners Hospital for children.

In the 7th grade I was able to shine and show the Computer Science Teacher what I already knew and was able to become the Teachers Aid.

In the early 2000's I was manipulating and creating XML's for a game, it was pointed out to me that this was coding(being able to know what parts did what and change them)

I have always been a quick learner with a good memory for what I learn, I have just always lacked the means to pursue my passion in computers. Until now.

My passion has been reignited and I have enrolled into a Game Developer/Full Stack Software Development Boot Camp at The Tech Academy in Portland OR.
I enjoy doing this type of stuff WebDesign/3D art/animation and creating various Documents/Spreadsheets/Maps and Programs/Functions.
I'm currently learning Python, C++, C#, HTML, CSS, JavaScript. I know its alot but its like I said "I'm a quick learner".
I'm also getting the chance to learn and work with alot of the current technolgy industry standard tools/software like Git, GitHub, IDLE, Visual Studio code, etc...
I'm also currently working With both the two Top Game Engines Unity and Unreal, and creating assets and models using Blender for 3D modeling.
My ultimate goal is to have a career in the Video Game Industry, or become a Software Developer(With a kick ass hobby of making video games on the side)
