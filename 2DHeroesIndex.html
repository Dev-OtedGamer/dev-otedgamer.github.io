2D Heroes – A Unity Game Project

Created in Unity 2021.3.45f1
This project was a major milestone in my Unity learning journey.

Project Overview

I developed this game while following a Unity tutorial, but I took it further by using my own tile sets, animations, and level designs. It was a hands-on way to learn about sprites, animation, pickups, level progression, and environmental storytelling.

Game Start & Editor View

Gameplay Features

These images showcase heart and diamond pickups, bombs, enemies, and hammer animations.

Level Design & Progression

I designed hidden paths and treasure areas to encourage exploration. In level two, I introduced difficulty progression using gravity and false perspective to create platforming challenges. The camera zooms dynamically, making timing and movement more intense. Even I struggle to beat it twice in a row!

Game Story

You've been taken hostage in the Pig Lair. Armed with your trusty hammer, you must battle your way out while reclaiming stolen treasure. How much can you recover before your escape?
