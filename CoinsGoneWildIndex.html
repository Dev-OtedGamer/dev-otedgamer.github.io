Coins-Gone-Wild - A Unity Game Project

Created in Unity 6000.0.31f1
Final assignment for the Unity course at The Tech Academy in Portland, OR.

Project Overview

This was a timed assignment to create a quick Unity game with a win condition. I spent too much time on ideas that were unattainable given the time limit. At the last minute, I pivoted to a coin collector game (thank you, Hedgehog, for the Hail Mary).

Level Design

I initially planned corridors and halls, but after adding jumping, I shifted to an island-hopping concept with water death traps. I tried making the cube roll or hop like a bunny, but it behaved unpredictably. I locked it to the ground and allowed jumping only from lower ground. I kept it simple due to time constraints.

Win Condition

Here's a snippet of the C# code for coin collection and the win condition. I was focused on submitting on time, so I didn’t create a win screen or loss condition. The win is logged in the console, and the game returns to the start screen upon winning.

Game Story

Unfortunately, there’s no story—just a coin collector. But I did learn that even a simple game requires thoughtful planning. Sometimes, even when you plan for something, it doesn’t work out and has to be cut for the project to move forward and meet the deadline. Always strive to under-promise and over-deliver—but above all, always deliver what you promise.
